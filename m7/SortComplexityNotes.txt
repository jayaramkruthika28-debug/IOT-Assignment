Sorting Algorithms Time and Space Complexity

1. Bubble Sort
Best Case: O(n)
Average Case: O(n^2)
Worst Case: O(n^2)
Space Complexity: O(1)
Stable: Yes

2. Selection Sort
Best Case: O(n^2)
Average Case: O(n^2)
Worst Case: O(n^2)
Space Complexity: O(1)
Stable: No

3. Insertion Sort
Best Case: O(n)
Average Case: O(n^2)
Worst Case: O(n^2)
Space Complexity: O(1)
Stable: Yes

4. Merge Sort
Best Case: O(n log n)
Average Case: O(n log n)
Worst Case: O(n log n)
Space Complexity: O(n)
Stable: Yes

5. Quick Sort
Best Case: O(n log n)
Average Case: O(n log n)
Worst Case: O(n^2)
Space Complexity: O(log n)
Stable: No
